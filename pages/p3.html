ABOUT

About

NPI is offering the physiotherapy world easy access to scientific research through a newsletter that summarises articles to the most important points. Their platform is used daily by physios that want to be on top of the new research published in their field.

Challenge

Every day, new research is published in the physiotherapy field and this makes it impossible for people to stay on top of the discoveries. The platform’s design is outdated and the challenge is to redesign the NPi platform so that it will engage more users and grow their base. Draw inspiration from learning devices such as Duolingo to include gamification.

Solution

I redesigned the service to become an application where users can use gamification to try out new concepts on digital models of muscles or joints. I added achievements, friend requests and a feed where users can check out the latest research, ask questions and get together to network.

Methods

I decided on the concept using one diverging and one converging ideation method. I used an evaluation matrix to decide which idea seems more feasible from a time perspective.

Methods

The concept was to create a gamification app where the user can test the concepts he learned on a live version of the part of the body. The scientific and research articles would be transcribed to a digital animation of the body.
