Team

The Municipality of Delft needs to transition to gas-free heating before 2050. The government’s immediate goal is to inform its residents about the importance of the transition and give alternatives to gas heating.  As a university project, I worked in an agile setup with other designers to find a solution for the client. The clients of the project were real.
