ABOUT

Undesign Team

4 young designers move to The Netherlands to start their journey of becoming UX designers with no prior experience.

Team

There were two teams so far. Learning from the first experience, I changed the setup of the 2nd team and looked to add more illustration duties. As the first team disbanded and all members started their graduation projects, the 2nd team was formed with new energy and looking forward for an improved version of the pilot.

PROCESS

Goal

I am looking to increase empathy through storytelling and express artistically through this piece. The experience of moving to another country, making new friendships, meeting new people and facing a new culture shaped me in many ways and I want to express those feelings that I do not know how to put in words through a comic book.
