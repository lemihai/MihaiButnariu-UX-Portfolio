ABOUT THE PROJECT

SHELL and the Kraaijvanger Architects are building the biggest hydrogen facility in Europe. They need to cummunicate the building to the public through an experience that captures the green design of the building.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

The design and build of the Holland Hydrogen 1 is almoust finished and with this, Shell needs to communicate the building to the public in an interactive and engaging way. The design of the building is unique, it blends the green spaces and fauna around with the design of the building. As designers we need to find a way to interactively comunciate this design with the visitors of the facility.
